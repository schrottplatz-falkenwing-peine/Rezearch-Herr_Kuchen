# Complete Battle Rap Record - Herr Kuchen
## Comprehensive Tournament History & Results

### Personal Information
- **Real Name**: Marc-Philipp Müller
- **Birth Year**: 1996
- **Residence**: Frankfurt am Main
- **Active Since**: 2012
- **Label**: Creative Bakery
- **Crew**: Keine Crew (No Crew)
- **Battle Name Origins**: Originally "Lebkuchenpferdchen"

### Tournament Participation History

#### BRT 2013 (BattleRapTurnier) - Debut
- **Original Name**: Lebkuchenpferdchen
- **Performance**: Won first round against "Benjamin White" (10:0 victory)
- **Achievement**: Ranked #4 out of 1600+ participants after first round
- **Progression**: Won multiple rounds, received bye for intermediate round
- **Elimination**: Lost in 32nd Finals against Ampère (BRT 2013 winner)
- **Significance**: First major tournament appearance, established reputation

#### VBT 2015 (VideoBattleTurnier) - Breakthrough
- **Qualification**: Unexpected qualification video gained attention
- **Performance**: Reached 32nd Finals
- **Achievement**: Major breakthrough, gained wider recognition
- **Notable**: First time using "Herr Kuchen" name
- **Impact**: Established him as notable battle rapper

#### VBT 2016 (VideoBattleTurnier) - Controversial Exit
- **Qualification**: Submitted qualification for VBT Elite
- **Scheduled Match**: Was to face Yasuo in 32nd Finals
- **Withdrawal**: Announced withdrawal on Facebook before deadline
- **Stated Reason**: "No interest in participation, qualification was album promotion"
- **Real Reason**: Interview suggested other reasons for withdrawal
- **Reception**: Poor response from battle rap community

#### JBB 2017/2018 (JuliensBlogBattle) - Peak Achievement
**Qualification**: Accepted into tournament via qualification video

**Tournament Progress**:
- **16th Finals**: Defeated Ti Music
- **Quarter Finals**: Defeated favorite Krickz (major upset)
- **Semi Finals**: Defeated Nairo (clear victory)
- **Semi Finals**: Defeated Grinch Hill (exploited weak round due to GreeeN tour)
- **Finals**: Lost to Timatic 4:11 (defeat in both rounds)

**Final Performance Analysis**:
- **Score**: 4:11 loss to Timatic
- **Controversy**: Herr Kuchen criticized tournament and Julien in HR
- **Speculation**: Believed Julien judged him more harshly due to criticism
- **Achievement**: Reached finals - highest career achievement
